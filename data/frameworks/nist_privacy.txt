NIST Privacy Framework
Version 1.0
Published: January 2020
Issued by: National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST)

Overview
The NIST Privacy Framework provides a voluntary tool to help organizations manage privacy risks related to personal data processing.

The framework aligns privacy practices with enterprise risk management.

Core Functions

1. Identify-P
Understand the organization's data processing environment.

Activities include:
• Data inventory
• Mapping data flows
• Identifying privacy risks

2. Govern-P
Establish governance structures and policies for privacy management.

Includes:
• Privacy policies
• Roles and responsibilities
• Oversight mechanisms

3. Control-P
Implement technical and procedural safeguards to manage privacy risks.

Examples:
• Encryption
• Access controls
• Data retention policies

4. Communicate-P
Ensure transparency about data processing activities.

Includes:
• Privacy notices
• User consent mechanisms
• Clear communication of data usage

5. Protect-P
Implement safeguards to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure of personal data.

AI Relevance
AI systems should ensure:
• Training data does not violate privacy laws
• Data is anonymized or pseudonymized when possible
• Automated decision systems remain transparent and auditable
• Organizations maintain accountability for AI-driven processing
