ISO/IEC 27701:2019
Privacy Information Management System (PIMS)

Issuing Organization
International Organization for Standardization (ISO) and International Electrotechnical Commission (IEC)

Overview
ISO/IEC 27701 extends ISO/IEC 27001 and ISO/IEC 27002 to provide a framework for managing personally identifiable information (PII).

The standard defines requirements for organizations acting as:
• PII Controllers
• PII Processors

Core Objectives
• Protect personal information
• Establish privacy governance
• Ensure transparency and accountability
• Implement privacy risk management

Key Controls

1. Privacy Governance
Organizations must establish privacy policies, roles, and responsibilities.

2. Data Subject Rights
Systems must support rights including:
• Access
• Correction
• Erasure
• Data portability
• Restriction of processing

3. Privacy Risk Assessment
Organizations must conduct privacy impact assessments to identify risks associated with processing personal data.

4. Data Minimization
Only necessary personal data should be collected and processed.

5. Secure Processing
PII must be protected using controls such as:
• Encryption
• Access control
• Secure storage
• Monitoring and logging

AI Relevance
AI systems must ensure:
• Responsible use of personal data
• Proper anonymization or pseudonymization
• Transparency regarding automated processing
• Privacy risk assessments before deployment
